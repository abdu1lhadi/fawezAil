about us

FTE Logistics is a %100 Saudi-owned company that started in 1998 as a small inland transportation establishment which has grown up to become one of the leading providers of heavy haulage and special transportation projects in Middle East with total logistical solutions. The headquarter is in Dammam which helps accommodate the administration office, vehicle workshop and container storage depot. Vehicles are also operated from a base in Jeddah and Riyadh, which caters for the growing levels of internal movements of various types of cargo.
