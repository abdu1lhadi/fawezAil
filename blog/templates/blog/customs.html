CUSTOM CLEARANCE

We work with national customs to offer a tailor made service suiting all clearance regulations and procedures. We provide custom clearance from all port in Saudi Arabia for all our clients and arranging applications for duty, exemption preparation, chemical permits and attestation of documents (such as certificates of origin from chamber of commerce or embassies) and ensuring a smooth process for your shipments throughout the transportation. We are also providing customs clearance service at Saudi Arabia borders for the cargo exported or imported from neighboring country.

Our promise is to develop timely solutions for customs clearance while clearance and delivering goods safely and in good condition.
