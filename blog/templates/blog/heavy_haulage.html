HEAVY_HAULAGE

FTE heavy lift transports heavy haul and over-dimensional loads and is the market leader in the heavy transportation and lifting industry in Saudi Arabia and Middle East. Regardless of the terrain, thousands of tonnes of machinery & equipment have been moved by our heavy haulage team since our inception & that number is always growing...

We have the capability to ensure the entire operation is taken care of, including the use of escort vehicles and vessels. Our core services include:
  • Heavy and abnormal lifts and load movements
  • Mobilisation and demobilisation of cranes
  • Route surveys and site inspections
  • Container deliveries
  • Accredited pilot services
  • Transport engineering
  • Disassembly and reassembly of equipment when required
  • Transport for complete pieces of equipment (no disassembly required) for certain kinds of heavy equipment and machinery
  • Services to, from and within metropolitan and remote areas
Equipment available consists of:
  • Tri Axle Low Low Loaders
  • Flat Top Trailers
  • Pilot Vehicles
